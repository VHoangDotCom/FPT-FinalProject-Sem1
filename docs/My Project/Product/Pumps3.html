This pre-fabricated system is designed for irrigation systems up to 150 gallons per minute and supplying pressures of 65psi.

The BT series “S” booster pump systems comes fully plumbed, wired, and mounted to a carbon steel frame powder coated safety blue. Each pump system comes furnished with isolation valves, check valve off the discharge line, and discharge pressure gauge for BT series that include VFDs. All control panels that come with the BT series pump package will we UL 508A listed with a Nema 4 panel, main electrical disconnect, power distribution and surge protection for each electrical component in the package.

  • Self-Priming Design
  • Fully Serviceable
  • FDA Compliant Impeller
  • Corrosion Resistant
  • Powered for Continuous Operation

Standard Features:

  • Variable Frequency Drive with Pressure Transducer
  • Nema 4 Control Box UL 508A Listed
  • Single Point Power Distribution and Disconnect
  • Surge Protection
  • Check Valve on pum discharge line
  • Isolation Valve
  • Pre-plumbed in K-Type Copper
  • Skid-Mounted on a Carbon Steel Skid and Frame Powder Coated Safety Blue
  • Pressure Gauge on discharge line
  • Flanged Inlet and Outle

Optional Features:

  • Pressure Tank
  • 100-Micron Disc Filter
  • Secondary Water Connection Motorized Valve
  • RPZ
  • Marine Grade Aluminum Enclosure with Two lifting handles, lockable latch, and cushion closed panel
  • Plumbing Options: Sch. 80PVC, L-Type Copper, Stainless-Steel

*The addition of the options above will change the overall dimensions of the skid.
