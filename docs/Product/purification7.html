The flagship unit in Viqua's popular rackmount line of water filtration and sterilization packages, the IHS22-D4 handles a robust 22 gpm flow rate and features both its sediment and carbon in 20" models for greater effectiveness and lower maintenance. The IHS22-D4 is the premier filtration and sterilization solution for home and light commercial applications.
