Specifications

This pump can be used for residential or commercial applications requiring higher pressures. i.e. water circulation, booster service, spraying systems for irrigation, general purpose pumping

The HSC is quiet and vibration-free and will provide trouble-free service for years to come. This pump can be mounted horizontal or vertically and with the compact design, this pump offers a versatility and adaptability for a number of uses. This pump can be taken apart my removing only four bolts which lessens the time of troubleshooting and maintenance. This pump is tested to UL778. Please note, this pump has a lead time of 1-2 weeks.

The HSC pump's impellers are 20% glass-filled thermoplastic precision molded for high efficiencies. The pump shaft is made from stainless-steel and the hex design provides positive drive for impellers and eliminates clearance adjustments. The motor was designed for continuous operation by incorporating a close-coupled design and is available from 3/4 - 3 HP.

Pump: up to 50gpm, up to 100psi (230ft), 1-1/4" suction pipe connection, 1" discharge pipe connection, temperatures to 180 degrees fahrenheit, maximum working pressure of 125psi Motor: NEMA Standard, 3/4 - 1-1/2 HP, 115/230V; 2 and 3 HP, 230V, 60 Hz., Single Phase ODP, Three Phase available, 3500 RPM, built in overload with automatic reset, cpacitator type
