The MQ is a small, easy-to-install booster pump that is suitable for rainwater harvesting applications.

Available in either 3/4 or 1 horsepower as well as 115V or 230V. Indoor or outdoor installation is possible, though a cover is needed if moisture will be present.

  • All-in-one pressure booster unit
  • Easy to install
  • Easy to operate
  • Self-priming
  • Dry-running protection
  • Low-noise
  • Minimal maintenance required
