Description

White 5,025 Gallon Below Ground Holding Tank, includes attached riser with 24-3/4" H x 25" inside diameter

Dimensions: 211" L x 102" W x 96" H

Part No.: 44876 / 44877* (*includes molded-on extension; Add $105 to price for molded-on extension)

Ships From: St. Bonifacius, MN, Tooele, UT, and Fairfield, TX

Norwesco holding tanks are suitable to store both potable and non-potable water and can be used as large capacity septic tanks. The integral columns inside the tank and the ribbed design ensure structural strength and durability providing peace of mind. These tanks are capable of accepting up to 4" plumbing options. The maximum burial depth is 30" for 2,600 and 2,650 gallon capacities and 24" for the remaining.

Norwesco below ground holding tanks are manufactured by means of the rotational molding process which produces a one-piece, seamless, watertight tank.

  • HDPE (High Density Polyethylene) construction
  • Manway size: 24", quantity 2
  • Lifetime corrosion warranty
  • Standard fittings: 2" bulkhead inlet and 2" bulkhead outlet installed
  • Includes threaded lid, 12" riser, riser gasket
